Welcome to the MET Museum Drawings and Prints Semantic Network.

Explore the interactive canvas below. Nodes represent 44 masterpiece drawings and prints from the German Renaissance (Gold), Dutch Golden Age (Amber), and French 19th-Century (Crimson). Edges connect artworks sharing visual features (Palette, Concepts, Mood, and Themes) computed using Gemini 3.5 Multimodal Vision-Language Model (gemini-3.5-flash).

  • Interact: Click and drag nodes to explore relationships, or use your scroll wheel to zoom.
  • Hover Nodes: View full historical metadata, image thumbnails, color palettes, moods, and latent themes!
  • Hover Edges: View the overlapping semantic tags bridging the two works of art.
